Look Ahead!

In June of 2024, I released "Look Ahead!" It is an EP based around the idea of an RPG following three kids and their journey up a mountain. The art was done by my friend (@portalpilled), and the full EP was made in about a month or so. It is video game inspired, specifically by Celeste, with a mix of some chiptune songs and other styles.It is pretty short, but it's worth checking out! (If you want, you can listen below, as well as on Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and many other platforms.)


Lonely Space Bunny Music

In November 2024, I released a second, shorter EP of five songs. A compilation of tracks I made after making Look Ahead. I wanted a smaller undertaking with a more concrete concept. It is about a lonely space bunny, hence the name. She is called Amie. She is alone, on a space station, and she has been alone for a long time. You can listen to it below (If you want, you can listen below, as well as on Spotify, SoundCloud, Bandcamp, and many other platforms.)
